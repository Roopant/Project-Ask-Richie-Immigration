Express Entry

Designed to select the most qualified candidates catering to Canada’s labor market, the Express Entry system provides the most promising, popular, and one of the fastest ways to Canadian Immigration.

What is Express Entry?

Canadian Permanent Residency is a dream for many, and Express Entry is one of the most promising and sought-after systems to make this dream a reality.



Family

Canada and ICCRC allow you to sponsor your spouse, common-law partner, or dependent child for Canadian Permanent Residence with its family reunification objective.

SPOUSAL SPONSORSHIP

Spousal Sponsorship Program is one of the programs under the Family Class Immigration category. It has two categories depending on your location – Inland and Outland Sponsorship.
